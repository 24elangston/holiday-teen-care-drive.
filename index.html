Holiday Teen Care Drive

Making a difference this holiday season for teens in need

Support Teens in Philly

This holiday season, we’re calling for your support to help teens in Philadelphia who are struggling with basic needs. Personal hygiene items, socks, scarves, and other essentials can make a world of difference.

Your generosity can bring warmth and comfort to these teens during this special time of year. You can participate by donating care packages or making a monetary contribution. Remember, no donation is too small!

How You Can Help

  • Donate hygiene items, socks, scarves, and other essentials
  • Contribute through our Cash App link below
  • Share this page with others to spread the word

Donation Deadline

Please make your contributions by December 22nd. We’ll personally collect all items or you can send your donation directly to our Cash App.
